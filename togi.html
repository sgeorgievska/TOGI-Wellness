About Us

TOGI is proud to be the first yoga program to combine self-awareness and financial literacy. Our mission is to provide a solid financial education to the next generation by incorporating decision-making and relaxation techniques – skills not addressed by the typical school curriculum. This program utilizes both behavioural strategies and the power of yoga, in order to inculcate positive long- term habits that will compound year over year.

We want to offer young people the opportunity to learn, express, create, play and be mindful, with the goal of developing a rich reservoir of the 3 R's of life in facing their future endeavours:

Respect

TOGI offers an element of social and self awareness, and we empower our TOGIs to share their ideas and knowledge.

Responsibility

TOGI will cultivate awareness for children to help them make sound decisions and co-corricular life experiences while engaging in exercise.

Resilience

We want to offer the future generation the opportunity to learn, express, create, play and be mindful, with the goal of developing a rich reservoir of discipline and resilience in facing their future endeavours.


About Tam

I am a mother, wife, yoga practitioner and educator. Every day I try to improve the lives of the people around me. My career as a bank manager has allowed me to meet and assist a wide variety of clients, and it has also sharpened my social and business leadership skills. Five years ago, however, while attending a yoga class, I realized for the first time that two very important, but easily overlooked, subjects intersected.

These were: coping with daily stress and the demands that life makes; and acquiring a basic understanding of one’s personal finances. These subjects had never been taught to me as a child, and their absence encouraged me to become an educator. I was inspired and determined to teach young people how to create balance in their lives. Areas such as one’s financial commitments, relationships, and self-esteem are all enhanced by the practice of yoga. Yoga is more than just a practice, it is a way of life. It has chosen me, and has given me a mandate to share my discoveries and talents with others.

We at TOGI believe that practising mindfulness will improve our lives and enrich the lives of our children.

Services

All services are mobile and travel fees vary depending on your location.

Private Classes

Want to compound on your children's awareness and life learning skills but do not have the time? Not to worry, TOGI offers a mobile service that specializes to your family's convenience. We bring the one on one or family setting classes to you.

School Events

We bring the goodness of healthy and mindful learning to your students. Children are not taught this much needed subject of financial literacy at any level. We help children and teens from all walks of life to learn, play and create while engaging in physical movements and exercise.

Birthday Parties

It's your child's special day, make it memorable and worth every penny spent. We bring fun games and activities suited to the birthday theme. TOGI makes every child's birthday party an upbeat event.

Festivals

Let's compound on the fun and create a one of a kind event with TOGI's yoga session. Nothing to worry about on your special day, we come prepared with learning props, music and a very energetic practice for all your special guests.

Daycare

Nothing less for our toddlers when it comes to mindful learning. A special program designed specifically for younger children who are so curious with learning. We offer a customized program for toddlers to build on interest with TOGI.

Community Clubs

Would you like to build on teamwork and build for the future? Our program offers an in depth perspective focusing on team work making the dream work. We are thrilled to come and share this knowledge at your centers.
